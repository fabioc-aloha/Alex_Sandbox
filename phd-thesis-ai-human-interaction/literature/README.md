## Search Strategy

### Primary Search Terms

```text
("artificial intelligence" OR "AI" OR "machine learning" OR "ML")
AND
("trust" OR "reliance" OR "collaboration" OR "interaction")
AND
("human" OR "user" OR "decision-making")
```

### Secondary Search Terms

```text
("explainable AI" OR "XAI" OR "interpretability" OR "transparency")
("appropriate reliance" OR "trust calibration" OR "over-reliance" OR "under-reliance")
("human-AI teaming" OR "human-machine collaboration")
```

## Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria

### Inclusion

- Empirical studies on human-AI interaction
- Theoretical frameworks for trust in AI
- Design studies for AI transparency
- Enterprise/organizational AI adoption

### Exclusion

- Pure technical AI papers without human component
- Studies on autonomous vehicles (separate domain)
- Non-peer-reviewed sources
- Studies pre-2015 (pre-modern AI era)

### 1. Trust in Automation/AI

- Classic trust models (Lee & See, 2004)
- Modern AI trust frameworks
- Trust calibration and repair

### 2. Explainable AI (XAI)

- Explanation types (local vs global)
- User-centered explanations
- Effectiveness of explanations

### 3. Human-AI Collaboration

- Team cognition with AI
- Role allocation
- Complementary performance

### 4. Organizational Adoption

- AI implementation barriers
- Change management
- Training and skill development

### 5. Responsible AI

- Fairness, accountability, transparency
- AI ethics principles
- Governance frameworks
