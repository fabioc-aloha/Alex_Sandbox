## Citation Style

**APA 7th Edition** (American Psychological Association)

### In-Text Examples

```text
Single author:      (Smith, 2023)
Two authors:        (Smith & Jones, 2023)
Three+ authors:     (Smith et al., 2023)
Direct quote:       (Smith, 2023, p. 42)
Multiple sources:   (Jones, 2022; Smith, 2023)
```
