# Episode Structure Template

Standard format for each episode.

---

## Cold Open (0:00-0:30)

Start with your strongest joke. No intro, just straight into comedy.

```
[HOSTS at desk]

HOST 1: [Best headline of the week + killer joke]

HOST 2: [React/Add-on]

[TITLE CARD]
```

---

## Intro/Titles (0:30-0:45)

Brief, punchy intro. Establish the show.

```
HOST 1: Good evening, I'm [Name].

HOST 2: And I'm [Name].

HOST 1: Welcome to [Show Name].

HOST 2: Let's get into it.
```

---

## Headlines Block 1 (0:45-6:00)

The main stories. Alternate hosts leading.

### Story Structure

```
HOST: [Setup - What happened]
      [Context - Why it's absurd]
      [Punchline - The joke]
      [Optional: Tag - Additional joke]

CO-HOST: [React/Banter/Follow-up joke]
```

### Typical Flow

1. **Lead Story** (Host 1) - 90 seconds
2. **Quick Hit** (Host 2) - 20 seconds
3. **Story 2** (Host 2) - 60 seconds
4. **Quick Hit** (Host 1) - 20 seconds
5. **Story 3** (Host 1) - 60 seconds

---

## Segment Break (6:00-9:00)

Recurring bit that breaks up the headlines.

Options:
- Correspondent report
- Deep dive on one absurd topic
- Audience interaction
- Character bit
- "Expert" interview

---

## Headlines Block 2 (9:00-12:00)

More stories, maintaining energy.

1. **Story 4** (Host 2) - 60 seconds
2. **Quick Hit** (Host 1) - 20 seconds
3. **Quick Hits Rapid Fire** (Both) - 60 seconds

---

## Sign-Off (12:00-12:30)

End on a high note.

```
HOST 1: That's all for this week.

HOST 2: [Callback to earlier joke OR final quick hit]

HOST 1: I'm [Name].

HOST 2: And I'm [Name].

BOTH: Good night!

[END CARD]
```

---

## Graphics/Visuals

Throughout the episode, support jokes with:

- **Headline Graphics** - News-style lower thirds
- **Story Images** - Photo of subject with funny caption
- **Reaction Shots** - Cut to co-host reacting
- **B-Roll** - Footage related to story (if available)
