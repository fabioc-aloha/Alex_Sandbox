## The Comedy Premise Test

### Is it relatable?
*Can the audience see themselves in this situation?*

### Is it specific?
*Does it have unique details that make it memorable?*

### Is it visual?
*Can you picture the funny scenes?*

### Is there irony?
*Is there a contrast between expectation and reality?*

### Does it escalate?
*Is there room for the situation to get progressively worse/funnier?*
