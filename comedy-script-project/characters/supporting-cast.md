## Comic Ensemble Ideas

### The Best Friend
- Loyal but gives terrible advice
- Gets protagonist into/out of trouble
- Contrast to protagonist's personality

### The Love Interest
- Source of romantic comedy
- Can subvert expectations
- Has their own comedic moments

### The Boss/Authority Figure
- Oblivious, demanding, or eccentric
- Creates workplace comedy
- Source of conflict

### The Family Member
- Embarrassing parent/sibling
- Well-meaning interference
- Holiday/gathering chaos

### The Neighbor/Coworker
- Quirky side character
- Recurring bit part
- Scene-stealer potential

### The Expert/Mentor
- Unconventional wisdom
- Unhelpful help
- Deadpan delivery
